# Education Center SaaS - Business Goals

## Mission

Help education centers replace Excel, paper records, and manual operations with a simple, modern, and automated management platform.

Every feature must solve a real business problem and save time, reduce mistakes, or increase revenue.

---

# Product Principles

Before implementing any feature, ask:

- What problem does this solve?
- Who benefits?
- Does it reduce manual work?
- Does it increase revenue?
- Does it improve parent satisfaction?
- Can the feature be simpler?

If the answer is "No", do not build it.

---

# Core Business Problems

## 1. Tuition Management (Highest Priority)

Pain

- Tuition tracked in Excel.
- Difficult to know who has paid.
- Manual lesson counting.
- Outstanding payments forgotten.
- Multiple tuition plans.

Business Value

- Reduce accounting workload.
- Prevent revenue loss.
- Improve cash flow.

Solution

- Automatic tuition calculation.
- Remaining lesson tracking.
- Outstanding balance management.
- Payment reminders.
- Payment history.
- Online payment support.

Priority: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

# MVP Scope (3 Months)

Build only the features that solve the biggest business problems.

Included:

- Authentication
- Dashboard
- Students
- Teachers
- Classes
- Courses
- Scheduling
- Attendance
- Tuition
- Notifications
- Reports
- CRM

Not Included:

- Online exams
- LMS
- Video learning
- AI
- Mobile apps
- Electronic signatures

---

# Success Metrics

A successful product should enable a center to:

- Register a student in under 2 minutes.
- Record attendance in under 10 seconds.
- Collect tuition in under 1 minute.
- View real-time revenue.
- Reduce manual administrative work by at least 50%.
- Improve parent communication.
- Increase student retention.

---

# Development Philosophy

Business first.

Every feature should accomplish at least one of these goals:

- Save time.
- Reduce mistakes.
- Increase revenue.
- Improve customer satisfaction.
- Reduce operational costs.

Avoid building features that do not deliver measurable business value.
